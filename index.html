About Us

We are a team called Nutrition Lab. We have been in the field of nutrition for over 5 years. The main philosophy of our team is that the result should depend not only on your physical efforts, but also on your psychological balance. That is why our programs include consultations on the psychology of eating behaviors, corporate lectures on selected topics, consultations with other specialists if necessary, etc.

Our programs are designed in such a way that you do not have to suddenly switch to proper nutrition. After consultation with our specialists, you will be selected the level of the program and training will be given all the necessary information and access to your personal account on out platform to track your progress.
